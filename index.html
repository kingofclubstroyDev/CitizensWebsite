01 --- Cyber City

Cyber City

Citizens from Terra seek a new chance at life in Yugen City. A floating cyber city in the clouds that provide a new digital life to escape from the physical reality. A “paradise” … if you will.

Some seek fame, some seek fortune… but seek revenge or power. All seek a new beginning. Some will find their new life to be everything they dreamed, yet some will succumb to the darkness hidden in Yugen’s seedy underbelly.

02 --- Tao

Tao

Yugen holds a very deep meaning of being a profound and mysterious beauty in the universe. Yugen is the sad beauty of human suffering, a beautiful acceptance of one’s fate and place in the universe. One accepts that place whether it be a state of bliss or one of pain.

8,888 citizens will be granted an opportunity in the cyber city of Yugen to experience this beautiful state of being in the universe. An ability to define who you really are, some will be wildly satisfied with this – yet some will fall quickly to the cleverness of man.

Will you connect with your tribe, or will you get lost in the darkness?

01

Yugen citizenship will be afforded to 8888 lucky people. Citizens will be able to participate in an evolving story, which travelers will only be able to observe. Citizens will choose between alliances with several factions run by enigmatic leaders representing different sections of the population and government. The lines of this alliance will be grey at best, Yugen is a world of deceit where many will simply lose their way.

02

The story of Yugen will be recorded through written word and grand illustrations. These works will only be available to Yugen Citizens and will be distributed digitally through Cyber City. Citizens of Yugen will feel immersed in the city itself as they will have the ability to form and shape the story as it is developed through their actions. A community built and owned brand.

03

The story as told per the events which occur in step 02 will inspire an animated series, which will tell the story of Yugen in an entirely new light. Citizens will have a direct impact on these events as they will be based both on the minted avatars, and on the events that occur around Yugen City.

04

Further immersion into the city will occur and develop, as the community chooses its own path. Companion art, intersection between the physical and digital world, and web2 collaborations are all on the table as directed by the community.
